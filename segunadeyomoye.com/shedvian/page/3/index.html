Story Of Love

Therefore a man leaves his father and mother and embraces his wife. They become one flesh.

I'm Shedrack

2014 is a year to remember, the year I met the one God had prepared for me. I met her in a fellowship meeting and again in the faculty where we both studied. We started talking. At one point, we met in the “love garden” where we talked about service and about the word. I invited her to a conference where I was going to be ministering and she accepted to come. I had nudges in my spirit telling me that we both had a walk together but I did not speak to her about it, not until 29th June 2014 . By August 2014 she accepted. We have been together since then, and the journey has honestly been one of faith, from the beginning, up until now, and even until tomorrow.

I'm Vivian

Our story might not seem like the perfect love story, but it is the perfect journey of faith. I’d say we met through a mutual friend - the Holy Spirit. He invited me to a meeting that he said he would be teaching at. We began talking even more consistently from the time. At the time I kept having nudges in my spirit that we had a path together, I later found out that he was having similar nudges as well but did not disclose them. He just kept calling and we kept talking. He straight up asked for us to start a relationship, I asked him to give me time even though I already knew what my answer was. All I have to say now is “By Faith Vivian...by Faith Shedrack…”

The Wedding Day

Making sure to incorporate the different cultures, religions, and other elements, this will be the order of events for the wedding.

Main Ceremony

11.07.2020

Marriage

09.30am onwards

The processional will begin with the grandparents of the couple, flowing through their parents, the groom, the officiant of the wedding, the flower girl and ring bearer, and finally, it ends with the bride making her entrance.

Words of Welcome

12.30pm onwards

Once everyone is in place, the officiant will say a few words of welcome. He or she may thank the guests for bearing witness to the union, as well as welcome everyone to the venue and for the celebration

Photography

2.00pm onwards

the officiant will offer an introduction and some thoughts on marriage while pictures with the couple are being taken. This could be a brief recounting of the couple’s love story, words on what marriage means to the person, or a statement about what the ceremony represents.

Wedding Cake

3.30pm onwards

The wedding cake is a celebratory creation that is decorated with flowers or other items. The cake is served to guests at the wedding reception.

Wedding Party

4.00pm onwards

At the wedding party the officiant introduces the married couple for the first time. Then, the bride and groom lead the recessional back up the aisle.
